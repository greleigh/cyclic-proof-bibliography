Non-well-founded and Cyclic Proof Theory A Bibliography

This bibliography lists a collection of academic work on the subject of cyclic and non-well-founded proof theory and its applications, consisting of published papers, theses, and preprints.

This website uses the bibtex-js library to render items directly from Bibtex files. Please feel free to access the Bibtex files directly on Github.

The intention is for this bibliography to be comprehensive. Suggestions of missing items, or for corrections, are welcome: please submit an issue in the Github repository, or send an email to reuben.rowe@rhul.ac.uk.
